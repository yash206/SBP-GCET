About The Event

The primary objective of the conference is to provide opportunity for academicians, industry experts, and researchers belonging to diverse fields to engage in discussion on issues related to sustainable business practices.

There will be clarity on what should be the main priorities of the managers in future with respect to sustainability of business.The conference will provide a platform to discuss sustainable solutions for the contemporary business problems.
